About the Author

I’m Cameron Havlish, I am a sound design student at Michigan State University where I am part of the game design minor. Like most of us on this site, I have been an avid gamer since I was young, first playing on the Nintendo Gamecube and have since played countless games that has led to wanting to pursue the career path I am in now. I mainly play FPS games but have been known to enjoy RPG’s. Outside of gaming, I am a metal head and spend much of my time listening to anything and everything metal, I am also a huge car nerd which if you are interested in seeing the stuff I have worked on just click here to see my car blog where I post about the different projects I have worked on and the various events I go to.
